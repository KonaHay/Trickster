What is Trickster?

Trickster is your one stop shop for everything wakeboarding!
Started as a dissertation project by Kona Hay, it has be come so much more!
It is designed to connect wakeboarders and help beginners on their course.
Trickser is truely made for wakeboarders by wakeboarsers!
